Chicken Fryed Chedar Recipe

Super tasty Fryed Chicken Recipe That Are Crispy, Crunchy, and Delicious

The following ingredients of the recipe is serving 4:

Ingredients

  • 2 Chicken breast
  • 2 Cups of buttermilk/milk
  • 1 Packet ranch dressing mix
  • 2 Cups cheese cracker
  • Oils for frying

Preparation

  1. Chop chicken breasts into pieces.
  2. Combine chicken, milk and ranch packet in a large bowl.
  3. Crumble the cheese crackers into desired crumbs in a large ziplock pack.
  4. Place cracker crumbs into a medium bowl and coat each piece of chicken until fully covered.
  5. Heat oil to 350°F/175°C and fry chicken until crackers turn golden brown (3 min).
  6. Place on a paper towel to dry.
  7. Enjoy with a side of ranch or any sauce.
